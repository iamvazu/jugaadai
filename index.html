FAQ

Straight answers.

What exactly is Paperclip AI?

Paperclip is our proprietary orchestration engine — the conductor of your agent stack. It coordinates agents, manages memory states, routes tasks to the optimal LLM, and handles retries. Think of it as the operating system that everything else runs on.

What's the difference between OpenClaw and NemoClaw?

OpenClaw is our long-context reasoning engine — built for complex analysis, proposal generation, and multi-step thinking. NemoClaw is our multi-agent runtime — built for parallel execution, scraping pipelines, and real-time coordination. Most deployments use both, routed by Paperclip.

How does the revenue share work?

Every deal directly attributed to an AI-generated lead gets a 10–12% share to Jugaad AI. We set up attribution tracking in your CRM on day one. If our agent booked it, we get the share. If your team sourced it independently, it's 100% yours. No ambiguity.

Is my data safe? Where does it live?

All data is stored in AWS Mumbai (ap-south-1) by default. We are DPDP Act 2023 compliant. We never use your customer data to train any model. Enterprise clients can opt for on-premise deployment using Llama 3.3 or Mistral with zero data leaving your infrastructure.

Why not just use ChatGPT or a freelancer?

ChatGPT is a chatbot — it doesn't run autonomously, doesn't have persistent memory across leads, and doesn't understand Indian market context. A freelancer sleeps, quits, and doesn't scale. Jugaad AI agents run 24/7, remember everything, and are specifically tuned for Indian sales cycles.

What happens after the 3-month pilot?

If agents are generating ROI (they will be), you roll into a standard monthly engagement. If they aren't delivering — which has never happened — you exit cleanly. We own the code. You own the data and all learnings from the pilot.

Do I need a technical team?

No. We handle all engineering. You give us access to your CRM, WhatsApp Business account, and email infrastructure. A business-side owner who can give weekly feedback is all we need. No developer required on your end.

Can agents speak regional Indian languages?

Yes. We support Hindi, Hinglish, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Marathi, and Bengali via our Qwen 2.5 and Llama 3.3 fine-tuned models. Regional language agents outperform English-only agents in Tier-2 city outreach by 2–3x.
